History

Assyafaah Mosque actually 'derived' from 2 mosque namely; Naval Base Mosque and Jumah Sembawang Mosque who were demolished to give way to a better facilities and bigger mosque for the community.

Hence, Assyafaah Mosque was built to better attend to the needs of the community. Since then, Assyafaah Mosque has flourished and does so by keeping their values close.

CORE VALUES

Integrity, Unity, Quality, Proactive, and Respectful

VISION

Dynamic Mosque, Progressive Community

MISSION

To Establish a Centre of Excellence for Islamic Learning & Social Development Strategic

DIRECTION

To Promote Islamic Learning & Complement Social Development Programmes
